About

Guzel Turkiye was created for anyone who is in love with the beautiful country of Turkey and wish to learn more about it. Here, you are going to find several interesting things about Turkey, such as its culture and people. So welcome to the journey of discovering this amazing nation that has so much to offer the world; however, before you begin this journey please leave all the negativity that the media has present to you about Turkey and open your mind, heart and eyes to see the true Turkey.

“The real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes, but in having new eyes. ” -Marcel Proust



Here are some curious facts about Turkey:

  • Santa Claus was born in Patara, Turkey
  • It's a transcontinental country
  • World's biggest producer of hazelnuts, figs, apricots and raisins
  • Turkish people love tea
  • The famous Trojan Wars took place in western Turkey
