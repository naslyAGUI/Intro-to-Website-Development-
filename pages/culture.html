Culture

Turkish culture is so amazing. At various points in its history, the region has been inhabited by diverse civilizations including the Assyrians, Greeks, Thracians, Phrygians, Urartians, and Armenians. Each of these cultures left a little of their cultures with the Turkish people. One example of this is the Blue Mosque located in Istanbul. The Blue Mosque was once a church, then a synagogue, and lastly a mosque. Definetely Turkey has a great culture that anyone should be able to explore. Let's begin the exploration!

Traditional dance

Kolbasti

Kolbasti is one of the many traditional dances of Turkey. It was originally created in the 1930s in the seaport of Trabzon on the Black Sea coast of north-eastern Turkey .

Islam

Islam

In 2016 Islam was the major religion in Turkey comprising only 98.3% of the total population, and Christianity with 0.2%.

Traditional Food

Kebab

Kebabs are various cooked meat dishes, with their origins in Middle Eastern cuisine. Many variants are popular throughout Asia, and around the world.
