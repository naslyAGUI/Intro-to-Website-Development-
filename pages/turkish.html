Turkish Language

Turkic Languages

Turkish is the official language of Turkey and Cyprus. It was first spoken in Mongolia. It's estimated that Turkish has around 75 million native speakers. Germany has around 2 million Turkish speakers. Before 1928, the Turkish language was written in Arabic script, but it was later change to Latin Script. Turkish has been influenced by Farsi, Arabic, and French language, so if you speak one of these languages you might understand some of their words.

" If you talk to a man in a language he understands, that goes to his head. If you talk to him in his language, that goes to his heart . " -Nelson Mandela.

Lesson

Omer: Merhaba (Hello)
Nazli: Merhaba, benim adim Nazli. Sizin adiniz ne?(Hello, my name is Nazli. What is your name?
Omer: Benim adim Omer (My name is Omer)
Nazli: Memnum oldum (Nice to meet you)
Omer: Ben de memnum oldum. (Nice to meet you too)
Nazli: Nasilsiniz? (How are you?)
Omer: Tesekkur ederim, iyiyim. Siz nasilsiniz? (I am fine too, thank you. and how are you?)
Nazli: Tesekkur ederim, ben de iyiyim. (I am fine, thank you)
